Achieve the best results with our wide variety of Cuisine!

Bean Roll-Ups

Spread bean dip on a flour tortilla, top with shredded cheddar and roll up. Brush with vegetable oil and bake 10 minutes at 400 degrees F. Serve with salsa.

Mango Salsa Scoops

Toss 1 cup each diced mango & black beans with 1/4 cup diced red onion. Add 2 tablespoons each chopped pickled jalapenos & the liquid from the jar, chopped cilantro & lime juice. Season with salt. Serve in tortilla scoops.

Crackerwiches

Spread peanut butter on saltines. Top with thin strawberry slices. Drizzle with honey and top each with another saltine.
